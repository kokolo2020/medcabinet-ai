# MedCabinet AI Product Vision

## Vision
MedCabinet AI helps households understand, organize, locate, and safely manage the medicines they already own.

The long-term vision is a trusted household medicine intelligence layer that reduces waste, improves adherence, prevents accidental duplication, and helps families make better purchasing and storage decisions.

## Problem
Household medicines are commonly spread across cupboards, bags, drawers, and refrigerators. People forget what they own, where it is stored, when it expires, what it cost, and whether a medicine is appropriate for a specific need. This leads to duplicate purchases, expired stock, unnecessary spending, and avoidable safety risks.

## Product Promise
MedCabinet AI turns a physical medicine cabinet into a searchable digital inventory using photos, barcodes, and manual entry. It should make the answer to four questions immediate:

1. What medicine do we have?
2. Where is it stored?
3. When does it expire?
4. Where and at what price can it be replaced?

## Target Users
- Families managing shared household medicines
- Caregivers managing medication for children, older adults, or patients
- Individuals with recurring prescriptions or chronic conditions
- Travelers maintaining portable medicine kits
- Cost-conscious users comparing repurchase prices

## Product Principles
1. Safety before convenience
2. Clear confidence levels for AI-derived information
3. User confirmation for medical identity and dosage data
4. Fast scanning with easy correction
5. Privacy by default
6. Useful even without perfect data
7. Family-friendly collaboration
8. Actionable reminders rather than notification overload

## Ten-Year Direction
MedCabinet AI may evolve from inventory management into a broader medicine lifecycle platform that supports household stock, pharmacy discovery, price history, verified product information, refill planning, recalls, family permissions, and interoperability with health systems where legally appropriate.

## Success Definition
The product succeeds when users:
- Find a stored medicine in seconds
- Reduce duplicate purchases
- Dispose of fewer expired medicines
- Maintain more complete medication records
- Receive timely expiry and refill reminders
- Trust the system enough to use it regularly

## Non-Goals
MedCabinet AI is not a doctor, pharmacist, diagnostic service, or emergency response system. It must not replace professional medical advice or make unsupported treatment recommendations.
