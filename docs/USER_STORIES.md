# User Stories

## Inventory Capture
- As a user, I want to scan a medicine package so that key details are entered automatically.
- As a user, I want to correct extracted information before saving.
- As a user, I want to add a medicine manually when scanning fails.
- As a user, I want to record quantity, expiry date, purchase price, store, and storage location.

## Search and Discovery
- As a user, I want to search by brand, generic name, category, barcode, or notes.
- As a user, I want to filter medicines by location, owner, expiry status, and stock level.
- As a user, I want to see whether I already own an item while shopping.

## Expiry and Stock
- As a user, I want reminders before a medicine expires.
- As a user, I want low-stock alerts for frequently used items.
- As a user, I want to mark medicines as used, discarded, donated, or finished.

## Price and Repurchase
- As a user, I want to record where and for how much I purchased a medicine.
- As a user, I want to compare my previous purchase prices.
- As a user, I want to save preferred pharmacies and replacement options.

## Family and Caregiving
- As an owner, I want to invite family members with controlled permissions.
- As a caregiver, I want to know which person a medicine belongs to.
- As a family member, I want changes to remain traceable.

## Reporting
- As a user, I want to export my cabinet inventory to PDF.
- As a user, I want a cabinet health summary showing expired, expiring, duplicate, and low-stock items.
- As a user, I want to understand estimated wasted spending from expired items.

## Safety Acceptance Criteria
- AI-generated medicine identity is never silently treated as verified.
- Dosage and treatment recommendations are not generated from inventory data.
- Emergency symptoms always direct users to local emergency services or qualified clinicians.
